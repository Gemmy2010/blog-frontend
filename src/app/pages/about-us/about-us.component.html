About

Greetings! I'm Jemima Osoro, a professional proficient in a wide range of software and web development skills. Additionally, I take pride in being the creator of this website! Web development has been a passion of mine for a long time. Following the completion of my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, and subsequently earning a Masters in Information Systems, I dedicated substantial time to coding websites both for enjoyment and financial gain.


When I first started programming, I didn't enjoy it. I thought I'd never be able to become a real programmer. It felt like I was pretending. There always seemed to be new tools to figure out, and new problems to solve. Even though I have a degree, sometimes I felt like I was not very smart. How can anyone keep up with technology that keeps changing?
Luckily, I found that: - Writing about the topics I learned helps me understand my thoughts better.

That’s why this website is created. It’s not just for helping other people, because it has helped me first.

I will mostly focus on the Angular library because it does not have as large a community as ReactJs.
